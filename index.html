About

Join technology and mobility

The Audi TT is a two-door sports car produced by Audi since 1998, manufactured in Győr, Hungary, by Audi Hungaria Motor Kft, using bodywork that is produced and painted at the plant in Ingolstadt, Germany. It is available in two-seater coupé and roadster versions. In 2006 Audi announced the second generation of its compact sports car and in 2014 the third. In 2015, the third generation of the audi tt hit the market with a novelty in terms of technology, an all-electronic panel called by the manufacturer Virtual Cockpit. The panel, now replaced by a 12-inch high-definition screen, can be configured in two display modes. The first, more traditional, has a speedometer and tachometer in the foreground. However, these instruments can be made smaller to highlight the map of the GPS navigator, in the second mode.
